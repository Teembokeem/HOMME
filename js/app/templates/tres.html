Our app, VURS works to solve how music radio platforms generate song recommendations by asking the community to upvote. We believe that crowdsourced recommendations from users with relevant music knowledge would produce the most accurate suggestions. As project manager, I notated milestones, and worked alongside partners to ensure deadlines were practical and manageable during the week-long sprint, and as creative director, designed a simple theme that was culturally relevant and poignant. Our case studies and ideation sprints analyzed apps like Pandora, Spotify, among others.
